Blixi

Refreshing new colorful puzzle

Blixi is a relaxing puzzle game with beautiful neumorphic design and innovative fresh gameplay. Compete for the top ranks with players from around the world on the constantly updated leaderboards. Blixi is an ideal casual game, you can interrupt and continue your progress at any time.

Missing the days when games weren’t all about advertising and spending money? Blixi is your game then.


How to play

Swipe (Use arrow keys - macOS) & merge circles

Features

  • One finger control (iOS only)
  • Player records and ladder system
  • Challenging achievements
  • Random generated endless gameplay
  • Day and Night mode
  • No time limits and no internet required for you to play
Blixi is universal app and available for free on the App Store.
